Why wrap here

Wrap ETH into WETH without losing custody

WETH gives ETH the token interface many decentralized exchanges, lending markets, NFT tools, and contract-based DeFi flows expect. This page focuses on a clean wrap and unwrap experience, not a speculative trade.

Wallet-native from start to finish

You connect a non-custodial wallet, choose ETH or WETH, and sign the wrap transaction yourself. Funds stay under your wallet control throughout the flow.

Built for Ethereum DeFi

WETH follows the ERC-20 token standard, which makes ETH easier to use inside smart contracts that expect token approvals, balances, and transfers.

Live cost and timing context

Wrapping is an on-chain wallet action, so timing and cost depend on network activity. The app surfaces the live route and your wallet shows the transaction request before broadcast.

How it works

Wrap WETH in three wallet steps

Use the same route to wrap ETH into WETH or unwrap WETH back to ETH. The important decisions are amount, direction, and wallet confirmation.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect the wallet that holds your ETH or WETH. No exchange account or deposit flow is required.

2

Choose direction and amount

Select ETH to receive WETH, or switch the route to unwrap. The quote area keeps the amount and route details inside the app.

3

Review and sign

Check the gas prompt, token direction, and receiving asset, then approve the transaction from your wallet only when the details look right.

FAQ

Wrap WETH questions

01

What does Wrap WETH mean?

Wrap WETH means converting native ETH into WETH, the tokenized version of ETH used by many DeFi contracts. The underlying value tracks ETH, but the token format is different.

02

Can I unwrap WETH back to ETH?

Yes. The route can be reversed from WETH to ETH, and the wallet confirmation will show the direction before you sign.

03

Which chains and assets are supported?

This flow is centered on ETH and WETH, with available networks shown inside the app. Review the current network list on the supported chains page.

04

How long does wrapping take?

Timing depends on the network and wallet confirmation. Once submitted, the transaction settles according to the chain's current conditions.

05

What does it cost to wrap?

The main cost is network gas, displayed by your wallet before signing. Any live route detail is shown in the app, so avoid confirming if the prompt does not match your intent.

06

How should I check safety?

Verify the asset, direction, wallet prompt, and destination network. Wallet tools built around the wallet connection standard help you confirm only the transaction you intend to send.
