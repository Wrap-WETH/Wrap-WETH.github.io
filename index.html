What is Wrap WETH?

WETH is Wrapped Ether, an ERC-20 representation of ETH created when ETH is deposited into the WETH9 smart contract. The Ethereum Mainnet contract is listed on Etherscan.

How Wrap WETH works

  1. Open the routeUse the official WETH destination and keep the domain in view before connecting a wallet.
  2. Connect on EthereumSelect Ethereum Mainnet, connect a self-custody wallet, and keep some ETH unwrapped for gas.
  3. Choose ETH to WETHEnter the ETH amount; the WETH9 contract credits WETH against deposited ETH.
  4. Confirm on-chainReview the network, contract address, and wallet simulation, then sign only if everything matches.

Before you wrap

ERC-20 compatibility

WETH gives ETH an ERC-20 interface used by many DeFi contracts; Ethereum's ERC-20 docs explain the standard.

Reversible flow

Wrapping deposits ETH into WETH9; unwrapping reduces your WETH balance and releases native ETH through the same contract logic.

Address-first safety

On Ethereum Mainnet, the commonly referenced WETH9 address is 0xC02aaA39b223FE8D0A0e5C4F27eAD9083C756Cc2; verify it before signing.

Wrap WETH FAQ

What does wrap weth mean?

It usually means converting native ETH into WETH, the ERC-20 version of ETH used by many Ethereum apps.

Is wrap weth the same as swapping ETH?

It is not a market swap in the usual sense; ETH is deposited into the WETH contract and WETH is credited at the contract level, while gas is still paid in ETH.

Which contract should I check before I wrap weth?

On Ethereum Mainnet, verify the WETH9 contract at 0xC02aaA39b223FE8D0A0e5C4F27eAD9083C756Cc2 before signing.

Can I unwrap after I wrap weth?

Yes. Unwrapping reverses the WETH position back to native ETH through the contract or a wallet interface that supports it.

Do I need ETH gas to wrap weth?

Yes. Keep ETH in the same wallet and network for gas; WETH itself does not pay Ethereum transaction fees.
